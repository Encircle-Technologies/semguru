The Challenge: Going Beyond “Okay”.

What do you do when your control is working just about okay…. But you are hungry for near dramatic improvements? Improvements that have the potential to unlock an additional 15% of your net revenue.

  • You (triple) down on understanding purchase intent & sophistication of your buyers.
  • You go all in on crafting a site journey that gets out of their way as much as possible.

This is exactly what Drerowery.pl and Bahomas.com did.

60% of traffic to the Drerowery site did not view a product. And this held true for traffic across all channels.

Drerowery buyers were fairly savvy. They had shopped cookware before.

Informed by past tests run for its varied clientele, eNavi decided to zero in on 3 potential problem areas where the Drerowery website was concerned: navigation menu, homepage hero & sub-hero, and collection pages.
